Heritage

A 30-year-old factory
that refuses to advertise.

JuTe International Food (儒德素食) was founded in 1995 in Bade District, Taoyuan, Taiwan. We focus on vegan and ovo-vegetarian, allium-free products made with non-GMO soy: meat alternatives, vegetarian ham, ribs, meatballs, and furikake — primarily exported to North American, Australian, and Southeast Asian vegetarian channels.Legal: JuTe International Food Co., Ltd. · VAT 16037664

We don't chase mass channels. We don't buy ads. Our brand equity comes from 30 years of compliance history at this Taoyuan factory — ISO 22000 Food Safety Management System and HACCP certified, with Halal and Clean Label certifications planned.

To every international buyer, our promise is simple: transparency, traceability, audit-readiness. Full production records, QC documentation, and third-party certifications available upon NDA execution.

Bilingual Line

We removed language errors
from the shop floor.

The hidden cost of B2B procurement is rarely raw material — it's miscommunication-driven returns and rework. JuTe employs both Taiwanese and Vietnamese workers, but more critically, we institutionalize that decision.

That means:

  • Bilingual work ordersEvery work instruction printed in both Mandarin and Vietnamese — workers read in their native language
  • Bilingual QC recordsEach QC entry signed in both languages, ensuring the recorder genuinely understands the spec
  • Bilingual trainingNew employee onboarding materials in parallel languages — onboarding cycle reduced by 40%
  • Audit-readyBilingual records meet ISO 22000 traceability requirements; supports international buyer factory audits
